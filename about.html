About the College

Dhamma Nandi Buddhist College was founded in 2019 (B.E. 2563) with the aim of nurturing both monastic and lay students in the light of the Buddha’s teachings. The college seeks to cultivate wisdom, compassion, and mindfulness, while providing opportunities for higher education in Buddhist and secular studies.

Our college campus provides a serene and disciplined atmosphere suitable for spiritual practice and academic learning. Through our programs, students learn not only the theory of Dhamma but also how to apply it in daily life to create a peaceful and harmonious society.
